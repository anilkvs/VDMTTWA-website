Welcome to Visakhapatnam District Masters Table Tennis Welfare Association

We are passionate about promoting Table Tennis for all age groups. Explore our team, events, rankings, and more.

VDMTTWA is dedicated to encouraging the sport of Table Tennis among veterans. We believe age is just a number when it comes to passion for sports.

Our Events

We organize regular tournaments and ranking events to engage players and recognize their efforts through a transparent point system.

Growing Together

Our community of players, supporters, and volunteers work together to promote the sport and foster camaraderie among players.
