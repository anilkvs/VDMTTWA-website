VDMTTWA is dedicated to encouraging the sport of Table Tennis among veterans. We believe age is just a number when it comes to passion for sports.

ABOUT VDMTTWA

VDMTTWA is a community of passionate veteran table tennis players from Visakhapatnam, focused on organizing events, maintaining rankings, and supporting players above a certain age group.

Meet the dedicated individuals behind the association who work tirelessly to bring table tennis to the forefront for veterans in the district.
